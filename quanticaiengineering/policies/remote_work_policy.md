## Policy Statement

Quantic AI Engineering recognizes that remote work arrangements can increase productivity, improve work-life balance, and support business continuity. This policy establishes guidelines for employees working from locations outside traditional office environments.

### Purpose
This policy aims to:
- Define eligibility criteria for remote work
- Establish expectations for remote employees
- Ensure security and compliance standards are met
- Maintain team collaboration and company culture

### Scope
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees approved for remote work arrangements, whether permanent, temporary, or hybrid.

---

## Eligibility

### Position Suitability
Remote work may be appropriate for positions that:
- Can be performed independently
- Do not require regular physical presence in the office
- Have measurable deliverables and outcomes
- Can be effectively managed remotely

Positions NOT suitable for remote work include:
- Facilities and physical security staff
- Laboratory technicians requiring specialized equipment
- Reception and front desk personnel
- Roles requiring daily in-person collaboration

### Employee Requirements
To be eligible for remote work, employees must:
- Have completed their probationary period (90 days)
- Demonstrate strong performance in their current role
- Show ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- Have no active performance improvement plans
- Possess reliable internet connectivity (minimum 25 Mbps download, 10 Mbps upload)
- Have a suitable home workspace

### Application Process
1. Employee submits Remote Work Request Form to direct supervisor
2. Supervisor evaluates request based on business needs and employee performance
3. HR reviews for policy compliance
4. IT assesses technical requirements
5. Final approval granted by department head
6. Remote Work Agreement signed by all parties

## Work Arrangements

### Arrangement Types

**Fully Remote:** Employee works from home or approved remote location 100% of the time. May be required to visit office for specific meetings or events.

**Hybrid:** Employee splits time between office and remote locations according to an agreed schedule (e.g., 3 days office, 2 days remote per week).

**Temporary Remote:** Short-term arrangement for specific circumstances such as illness recovery, family care, or business continuity needs.

**Remote Days:** Occasional work-from-home days for employees normally working on-site (typically 1-2 days per month).

### Schedule Requirements
- Remote employees must maintain regular work hours agreed upon with their manager
- Core collaboration hours of 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (employee's local time) must be observed
- Schedule changes must be communicated 48 hours in advance when possible
- Time zone differences must be considered for meeting scheduling

### Geographic Restrictions
- Remote work must be performed within the United States or approved international locations
- Employees must notify HR of any permanent address changes
- Tax and legal implications of remote work location are employee's responsibility
- International remote work requires additional approvals and may not be permitted for all positions

## Equipment and Technology

### Company-Provided Equipment
The company will provide:
- Laptop computer with required software
- External monitor (for full-time remote employees)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls
- VPN access credentials
- Collaboration software licenses

### Employee Responsibilities
Employees are responsible for:
- Maintaining equipment in good working condition
- Reporting technical issues promptly to IT helpdesk
- Returning equipment within 5 business days of employment termination
- Not allowing family members to use company equipment

### Internet and Phone Service
- Employees must maintain reliable high-speed internet service at their own expense
- Company will provide monthly stipend of $75 for internet/phone for full-time remote employees
- Mobile hotspot data may be reimbursed for business travel

### Technical Support
- IT helpdesk available Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM ET
- Remote assistance provided for all company equipment
- Response time: 4 hours for critical issues, 24 hours for non-critical issues
- Extended support available during scheduled company events

## Security Requirements

### Data Protection
All remote employees must:
- Use company VPN for all work-related activities
- Enable full-disk encryption on company devices
- Store all work files on company-approved cloud storage (OneDrive, SharePoint)
- Never store sensitive data on personal devices or unsecured locations
- Lock computer when unattended

### Network Security
- Connect only to secure, password-protected Wi-Fi networks
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for work activities
- Use mobile hotspot if secure network is unavailable
- Do not connect company devices to potentially compromised networks

### Physical Security
- Keep company equipment secured when not in use
- Do not leave devices visible in vehicles
- Ensure family members and visitors cannot access company equipment
- Report lost or stolen equipment immediately to IT and Security

### Confidentiality
- Ensure confidential conversations cannot be overheard by household members or others
- Use private space for video calls containing sensitive information
- Do not discuss confidential matters in public spaces
- Properly dispose of printed confidential materials (shred or return to office)

### Access Controls
- Never share passwords or access credentials
- Use multi-factor authentication for all systems
- Log out of applications when finished
- Change passwords immediately if compromise suspected

## Communication Expectations

### Availability and Responsiveness
- Be available during agreed work hours
- Respond to emails within 4 business hours
- Respond to instant messages within 30 minutes during core hours
- Update calendar with availability, meetings, and time off
- Set status in communication tools (Teams, Slack) to reflect availability

### Video Conferencing Standards
- Keep camera on during meetings when possible
- Use appropriate background or virtual background
- Mute microphone when not speaking
- Test audio/video before important meetings
- Dress professionally for client-facing meetings

### Regular Check-ins
- Daily stand-up or check-in with team (format varies by department)
- Weekly 1:1 meeting with direct supervisor
- Participate in all required team meetings
- Proactively communicate progress, challenges, and blockers

### Tools and Platforms
Approved communication tools:
- Email: Outlook
- Instant messaging: Microsoft Teams
- Video conferencing: Teams, Zoom (for external meetings)
- Project management: Jira, Asana
- File sharing: SharePoint, OneDrive

## Performance Management

### Measuring Performance
Remote employees are evaluated on:
- Quality and timeliness of work deliverables
- Achievement of goals and objectives
- Communication and collaboration with team
- Adherence to policies and procedures
- Professional development and growth

Performance is measured by results, not hours logged.

### Documentation Requirements
Managers should:
- Set clear expectations and deliverables
- Document regular check-ins and 1:1 meetings
- Provide ongoing feedback
- Address performance issues promptly
- Conduct formal reviews per standard schedule

### Performance Issues
If performance concerns arise:
1. Manager discusses concerns with employee
2. Performance Improvement Plan may be implemented
3. Remote work privileges may be temporarily suspended
4. Return to office work may be required
5. Continued underperformance may result in termination

## Workspace Requirements

### Home Office Standards
Remote employees should establish a dedicated workspace that:
- Is quiet and free from distractions
- Has adequate lighting and ventilation
- Includes ergonomic furniture (desk and chair)
- Provides reliable power and internet connectivity
- Allows for professional video call backgrounds

### Ergonomics
- Use adjustable chair with lumbar support
- Position monitor at eye level, arm's length away
- Keep keyboard and mouse at elbow height
- Take regular breaks (5-10 minutes per hour)
- Request ergonomic assessment from HR if needed

### Workers' Compensation
Injuries occurring during work hours in the designated home workspace may be covered by workers' compensation. Employees must:
- Report work-related injuries immediately
- Complete incident report within 24 hours
- Maintain safe work environment

### Home Office Stipend
Full-time remote employees receive:
- One-time $500 home office setup allowance
- Monthly $75 internet/phone stipend
- Annual $200 office supplies allowance

Submit expenses through Concur with receipts for approval.

## Policy Modifications and Termination

### Trial Period
All new remote work arrangements include a 90-day trial period. During this time, the arrangement may be terminated by either party with minimal notice.

### Policy Changes
This policy may be updated at any time. Employees will be notified of significant changes and may be required to sign updated Remote Work Agreements.

### Termination of Remote Work Arrangement
Remote work privileges may be revoked if:
- Business needs change
- Performance standards are not met
- Policy violations occur
- Position requirements change
- Employee relocates to unsupported location

Employees will receive at least 30 days notice to transition to office work, except in cases of serious policy violations.

### Return to Office
If required to return to office:
- Equipment must be returned or brought to office
- Remote work stipends will cease
- Workspace will be assigned (may not be original workspace)
- Employee may reapply for remote work after 6 months
