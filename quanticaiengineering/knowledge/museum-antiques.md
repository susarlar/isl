# Museum Antiques & Trophies — Guild Guide

## 🎯 Quick Answer: Which Antiques To Max

**Max the antique that matches your main typing first. Each typing has exactly one dedicated antique:**

| Your Main Typing | Antique To Max | What It Gives |
|------------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Inspiring** | **Oyster Trap** (also called "Trapped Oyster") | Inspiring fellow aptitude + % Inspiring power on awake |
| **Diligent** | **Narwhal** | Diligent fellow aptitude + % Diligent power on awake |
| **Brave** | **Sledge** | Brave fellow aptitude + % Brave power on awake |
| **Informed** | **Bear-Eared Seal** | Informed fellow aptitude + % Informed power on awake |
| **Unfettered** | **Mammoth** | Unfettered fellow aptitude + % Unfettered power on awake |

**Then** push these three event-pass antiques that boost ALL fellows regardless of typing (so they're universally valuable):
- **Gold Poker** (Casino on Yacht event pass)
- **Medal of 15 Trials** (Cloud Kingdom event pass)
- **Mysterious Cauldron** (Penglai event pass)

**And** max these two excavation antiques for their SSR/UR targeting (only if you have them unlocked via Frozen Abyss / Ice Shipwreck caves):
- **Dragon** — UR(+) fellow aptitude + UR(+) fellow power %
- **Mural** — SSR(+) fellow aptitude + SSR(+) fellow power %

Spend **Gold points** (the highest upgrade currency) exclusively on your main typing's antique. Spend **Silver points** on the three all-fellow event pass antiques. Spend **Bronze points** freely across everything else. See the sections below for mechanics and caves.

## Two Separate Systems

| System | Source | What They Give |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Trophies** | Top 10 / 1st place in events + Glory Road | % boost to All Fellow Power |
| **Antiques** | Excavation minigame + event passes | Aptitude, flat power, and % power bonuses (typed) |

These are completely separate. Don't confuse them.

## Museum Trophies

- Earned by placing **1st or top 10 in event rankings**
- Provide **% boost to ALL Fellow Power**
- Has a **Glory Road** progression system — **don't forget to collect the crystals from Glory Road**
- Permanent collection items

## Museum Antiques (Excavation Game)

### How It Works
1. Play the excavation minigame to collect antiques
2. Use antiques to **unlock** entries first
3. Then **awaken** them with fragments
4. **Bronze → Silver → Gold** point upgrades (with diminishing returns at each tier)
5. Fragments come from **duplicate antiques** when you keep playing the minigame

### Antique Bonus Structure
Each antique can give:
- **Aptitude** to a specific typing
- **Flat power** bonus
- **% power** bonus
- An **awake effect** (unlocked via awakening) that triggers on milestone counters

### Per-Typing Antiques

| Antique | Typing | Base Effect | Awake Effect |
|---------|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Oyster Trap** | Inspiring | Inspiring fellow aptitude (level with coins) | Per 30 guild donations: +% Inspiring fellow power |
| **Narwhal** | Diligent | Diligent fellow aptitude | Per 300 trade post opponents defeated: +% Diligent power |
| **Sledge** | Brave | Brave fellow aptitude | Per 500 educations: +% Brave fellow power |
| **Bear-Eared Seal** | Informed | Informed fellow aptitude | Per 100 field trips: +% Informed power |
| **Mammoth** | Unfettered | Unfettered fellow aptitude | Per X study tour rewards claimed: +% Unfettered power |

### Event Pass Antiques

These are bought with event passes and ALL give a % boost to **All Fellow Power**:

| Antique | Source Event | Base Effect | Awake Effect |
|---------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Gold Poker** | Casino on Yacht | All Fellow Power % | All Fellow Aptitude boost |
| **Medal of 15 Trials** | Cloud Kingdom | All Fellow Power % | All Fellow Aptitude boost |
| **Mysterious Cauldron** | Penglai | All Fellow Power % | All Fellow Aptitude boost |

### Excavation Cave Antiques

Found by playing the excavation game in specific caves:

| Antique | Cave | Base Effect | Awake Effect |
|---------|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Mural** | Memory Cave / Frozen Abyss / Ice Shipwreck | SSR(+) fellow aptitude | SSR(+) fellow power % |
| **Dragon** | Memory Cave / Frozen Abyss / Ice Shipwreck | UR(+) fellow aptitude | UR(+) fellow power % |
| **Ironhead Turtle** | Frozen Abyss / Ice Shipwreck | SSR(+) aptitude | Open Celebration Banquets (free banquets) |
| **Abandoned Portal** | Excavation cave | SSR(+) aptitude | All Fellow Power % |

### Cave Typing Coverage

Each cave has different typing affinities — pick the cave that boosts your main typing:

| Cave | Typing Coverage | SSR Boost? |
|------|----------------|------------|
| **Memory Cave** | Informed / Unfettered | ❌ No SSR boost |
| **Frozen Abyss** | Unfettered / Diligent | ✅ SSR boost |
| **Ice Shipwreck** | Brave / Informed / Inspiring | ✅ SSR boost |
| **Original Ruins** | (general) | **GET ONE OF EACH AND GET OUT — NO Aegle/Dragon/Mural** |

### Treasure Hunt Strategy

When choosing where to hunt (after collecting everything at least once), **only hunt in the cave that has the antique of your main typing**.

## Antique Upgrade Currencies

You upgrade antiques in three tiers:
1. **Bronze points** (lowest tier upgrade)
2. **Silver points** (mid tier)
3. **Gold points** (highest tier)

**Diminishing returns** apply — each tier gives less per point spent than the previous, so spend bronze freely but save gold for your best antiques.

## Celebration Banquets (from Ironhead Turtle awakening)

A celebration banquet is a **free banquet** — different from regular banquets:
- **Regular banquet**: 4 people with beer and cheese
- **Wine/grape banquet**: 8 people with wine and grapes (twice as many points, participants get crystals)
- **Celebration banquet**: Free, opened by Ironhead Turtle awakening

## Strategy Notes

1. **Get ALL Original Ruins antiques but skip Aegle/Dragon/Mural there** — Original Ruins is for completion, not investment
2. **Stay in your main typing's cave** for ongoing hunting
3. **Save Gold points** for your main typing's antique
4. **Don't forget Glory Road crystals** — easy to miss
5. **Excavation game ticket = 1 free per day** (used for fragment generation)

## The Big Picture

Museum antiques are an underrated source of typing-specific aptitude and % power. They:
- Add to your fellow's aptitude pool (multiplied through the formula)
- Add to the Σ% pool when awakened
- Trigger awake effects from gameplay milestones (donations, education, etc.)

For your main typing, fully developing your typing-specific antique can add hundreds of aptitude and significant % power over time.
