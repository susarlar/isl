# Stella System — Advanced Guide

## What Stella Is

Stella is a **fellow/family upgrade system** that provides:
1. **Personal Power boost** to the character
2. **Group-wide Power boost** to all fellows in the same Stella group
3. **Building Earnings** boosts (some characters)
4. **Blessing Bond** unlocks (at Stella Lv.4 for some UR characters)

Stella is one of the strongest multipliers because it affects an entire group of fellows simultaneously.

## Stella Groups

There are **11 known Stella groups**. Each fellow belongs to exactly one group, and upgrading any member's Stella boosts the entire group.

## Stella Investment Strategy for Level 45+ Players

### Priority 1: UR Group Stella (If You Have UR Fellows)
- **Empyrean Sound** or **Ancient Magi** Stella gives +175% at Lv10
- Even Lv2 gives +55% — massive early value
- Focus fragments on one UR fellow's Stella at a time

### Priority 2: SSR Group Stella Matching Your Core Team
- If you run Archdemons → push any Archdemon's Stella
- If you run Elites → push any Elite's Stella
- Otherworld Valiants are extra valuable (cross-boosts Archdemons at Lv11+)

### Priority 3: Followers/Prominent Stella (Lower Priority)
- Only +25% at Lv10 — invest here after higher groups are leveled
- Exception: Jewlry (Followers) for the flexible blessing slots

### Key Insight: Group Size Matters
- Empyrean Sound: 4 fellows × 175% = massive ROI but narrow
- The Elites: 10 fellows × 50-100% = broader coverage
- Archdemons + Otherworld cross-boost: 10 fellows total with synergy

### Fragment Sources
- **Duplicate gacha pulls** → 100 Fragments per dupe
- **Event shops** → Some events sell specific fragments
- **Event rewards** → Limited availability
- Every "bad" dupe is Stella fuel — nothing is wasted
