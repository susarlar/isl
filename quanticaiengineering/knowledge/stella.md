# Stella System — Overview

> For complete level tables, costs, and member lists per group, see `stella-tables.md`.

## What Stella Is

Stella is a per-fellow upgrade system. Depending on the Stella pattern, individual levels can give any combination of:

1. **Personal power boost** (flat power added to the fellow)
2. **Group-wide power boost** (% to all fellows in the same Stella group)
3. **Fellow-specific power boost** (% bonus to just the one fellow at this Stella)
4. **Aptitude bonuses** — flat aptitude to the group (Pattern A) or skill cap raises
5. **Aptitude skill cap raises** — the game limits how much aptitude you can add to a fellow via Skill Pearls; Stella (Pattern A) pushes that cap higher so you can feed more Skill Pearls into the fellow and get more power overall. This is one of the most important endgame benefits of Pattern A Stella.
6. **New aptitude slots** — unlock additional aptitude-skill slots on the fellow (Pattern A)
7. **Building Earnings** boosts — extra % earnings when the fellow operates a building (Pattern A)
8. **Family Blessing unlocks** — at Pattern A Lv 4 specifically, a Family Blessing bond unlocks between the fellow and a family member (the exact family/fellow pairing varies per fellow)
9. **Level cap raises on fellow aptitude** (Pattern A)

The exact mix of these benefits per level is documented in `stella-tables.md`.

## How Stella Is Powered — Character Fragments & Shards

Most Stella groups need **per-fellow Character Fragments** (dupes of that specific fellow) to level up. A handful of groups use shared or event currencies instead:

| Currency | Group | Mechanic |
|----------|-------|----------|
| **Purple Stars** | Archdemons | Shared pool — any Purple Star can upgrade any Archdemon. Obtained from events, gacha, game shops — generally plentiful. |
| **Insignias** | Otherworld Valiants | Shared pool — any Insignia can upgrade any Valiant. Obtained similarly to Purple Stars. |
| **Magi Shards** | Ancient Magi | **NOT a single shared pool.** Each individual Magi (Hermes, Ao Li, Phanes, etc.) has their OWN Magi-specific shards. You cannot use Ao Li shards on Hermes, etc. Magi shards come from chests (random drops), purchases, and several other sources, but the per-fellow segregation makes Magi harder to spread than Archdemon/Valiant. |
| **Character Fragments** | All other groups | Each fellow needs their OWN dupes. Heavy bottleneck on personal dupe income. |

**Practical takeaway:** Archdemons and Otherworld Valiants are the easiest groups to spread Stella across because any currency can go to any member. Ancient Magi looks shared but isn't — it still requires per-fellow investment, just with a different naming scheme. All other groups are dupe-bottlenecked. Whether this makes Archdemon/Valiant more "efficient per Stella level" is not clearly established — it mainly makes spreading across the group easier, which matters more for group-wide % unlocks than for pushing any one fellow's personal Stella deep.

## Four Stella Patterns

"Alternates" below means **group boost on odd levels, fellow-specific boost on even levels** — the level-by-level rhythm for Patterns B and C.

| Pattern | Used By | Per-Level Rhythm | See `stella-tables.md` for |
|---------|---------|------------------|----------------------------|
| **A: Premium UR** | Empyrean Sound, Ancient Magi, Divine Gospel | Each level gives several of: flat power (+3–10M), group aptitude, group % boost, fellow % boost, building earnings %, skill cap raise, new aptitude slot. Flat power scales up across levels. | Empyrean Lv 1–20, Magi Lv 1–10, Divine Gospel Lv 1–10 |
| **B: SSR High** | Archdemon, Otherworld Valiants, Elites, Diligent Dynamo, Moonlit Visitor, Uncrowned, Greyrat Family, Dungeon Challenger | Alternates — odd levels: +1.5M power + group +10%; even levels: +1.5M power + fellow +30% | Otherworld Lv 1–20, Elites Lv 1–10, Dynamo Lv 1–6, Archdemon Lv 21–40 |
| **C: SSR Low** | Followers | Alternates — odd levels: +1M power + group +5%; even levels: +1M power + fellow +15% | Followers early levels |
| **D: SR Standalone** | Rani, Elise, Liz, Angie (no group affiliation) | One flat standalone fellow that gives a category-wide % boost (~+30% at max verified level) | Pattern D notes |

**Important caveat on Divine Gospel (Pattern A):** Divine Gospel membership is **VIP-gated** — most Divine Gospel fellows (Mammon, Trady, Mescal, Shlomo) are **only obtainable by paying for VIP levels**, and only Neptune is a "regular" UR in the group. Whales can convert Trady and Mescal from SSR into UR, but everyone else stays SSR. On top of that, Divine Gospel character fragments are **very rare**, making it hard to push Divine Gospel Stella even for players who have the fellows. For most F2P and mid-spender accounts, **Divine Gospel is effectively inaccessible** — plan around Empyrean Sound and Ancient Magi instead.

**Note on Pattern A numbers:** The old summary ("+175% power, +140 apt, +400% earnings at Lv 10") was derived from early verified levels on Empyrean Sound. The actual per-level breakdown is more complex and varies between Pattern A groups. See `stella-tables.md` for exact values.

## Critical Strategic Insight

**Brave and Unfettered have NO SR Category Stella booster.** This is a permanent ~30% multiplier gap if you main these types.

Inspiring uniquely has TWO Category Stella boosters (Rani + Elise), and **they stack fully** — both Rani's +30% and Elise's +30% add to the Σ% pool independently for **+60% total** to all Inspiring fellows. This is verified in-game: there is no cap on having multiple Category Stella boosters active for the same type. Inspiring carries therefore have the strongest SR-sourced multiplier stack of any typing.

## Data Gaps — Higher Stella Levels

All verified data in `stella-tables.md` comes from a single Inspiring-main account. Coverage by group:

- **Empyrean Sound (Pattern A):** Levels 1–20 verified
- **Ancient Magi (Pattern A):** Levels 1–10 verified; Lv 11+ unknown
- **Divine Gospel (Pattern A) — Neptune only:** Levels 1–10 verified. Other Divine Gospel fellows (Mammon/Trady/Mescal/Shlomo): Levels 1–7 verified in the Pattern B-like rhythm
- **Archdemons (Pattern B):** Lv 21–40 verified (120 shards/level). Lv 1–20 visible pattern is unclear because account has already activated all Archdemons
- **Otherworld Valiants (Pattern B):** Levels 1–20 verified
- **The Elites (Pattern B):** Levels 1–10 verified
- **Diligent Dynamo (Pattern B):** Levels 1–6 verified
- **Followers (Pattern C):** Levels 1–2 verified; rhythm inferred for 3+
- **Moonlit Visitor, Uncrowned, Greyrat Family, Dungeon Challenger:** No per-level data
- **Pattern D (Rani/Elise/Liz/Angie):** Max verified level ≈ 10

**Beyond these levels the exact values are not in this knowledge base.** Do not trust wiki numbers for high Stella — they're often wrong or outdated. If you have higher-level data or data on groups we don't cover, contact **Su** to get it added.

## Investment Priority

> **Caveat:** This priority order is based on reasoning about pattern strength and accessibility, not verified per-level ROI calculations. Numbers needed to calculate true per-level efficiency are only partially available.

1. **Pattern A — Empyrean Sound and Ancient Magi** — Highest ceiling by far (flat power at +3M–10M per level plus aptitude cap raises that feed into Skill Pearl scaling). These are the two groups worth concentrated investment on your main carry.
2. **Pattern B with shared currency** (Archdemons, Otherworld Valiants) — Shared currency makes it easy to spread Stella across the whole group, so the group-% unlocks come faster. Good value for effort.
3. **Pattern D (SR Category)** — Massive per-account value if you main **Inspiring, Diligent, or Informed** (the three typings with standalone SR boosters: Rani/Elise for Inspiring, Liz for Diligent, Angie for Informed). Brave and Unfettered have no Pattern D options.
4. **Pattern B with personal fragments** (Elites, Diligent Dynamo, Moonlit Visitor, Uncrowned, Greyrat, Dungeon Challenger) — Dupe-bottlenecked. Push only the specific members you care about.
5. **Pattern C (Followers)** — Lowest per-level values, push last.
6. **Pattern A — Divine Gospel** — Theoretically top tier, but VIP-gated membership and very rare character fragments make this inaccessible for most accounts. Skip unless you're a whale.
