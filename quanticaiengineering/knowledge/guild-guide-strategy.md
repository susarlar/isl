# Guild Guide — Master Strategy (Updated)

> Source: Guild guides, integrated into the knowledge base. Combined with verified power formula data.

## Choose Your Main Typing First

The current meta revolves around **picking ONE main typing** and giga-boosting that. All typings are viable depending on your playstyle. **Don't think it's ever too late to choose your main typing.**

When choosing, look at:
1. The main UR fellows for that typing
2. The events those URs come from (frequency + reward quality)
3. The family coverage available

### Recommended by Playstyle

| Playstyle | Typing | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **F2P** | Informed | Free Stella 10 Aegle, Aloha event is cheap |
| **F2P with alts** | Unfettered | Tree event with alts gives free Stella 10 Orivita + unlimited outfit upgrades |
| **Whale / Brave whale** | Brave | Olympics (Cloud Kingdom) lets you swipe directly on Heracles Stella |
| **Established Inspiring** | Inspiring | Only main if already invested — Guild Trade is too rare for new mains |

## Power Investment Order

Focus on these in order:

1. **Fellow** — Main carry levels, awakening, skill pearls
2. **Family** — Blessing on the families that bless your main carry
3. **Stella to Lv 4 (Fellow)** — Unlocks a Family Blessing pairing (specific to the fellow)
4. **Outfit to Lv 5** — Maximize aptitude/typing bonuses
5. **Stella Lv 6 → Lv 10 (Fellow)** — Depends on what you want:
   - **Stella 6**: Max operation earnings
   - **Stella 8**: Title Frame
   - **Stella 10**: Animated Avatar
   
   If you don't care about cosmetics, just continue getting outfits at Step 5 instead.

## Event Gacha Strategy

Once you have all the characters from a gacha banner, **look for outfits of your main typing and pull on them endlessly**. Outfit collection is the long-tail goal.

## The Meta Summary

Pick a typing → invest everything into that one typing's ecosystem (fellows, family, Stella, outfits, fish, artifacts). Don't spread your investment across multiple typings.
