## Advanced Investment Strategy for Level 45+ Guilds

### Single Carry Meta
1. Pick your highest-aptitude fellow (ideally 120 apt UR)
2. Stack ALL multipliers on them: Awakening, Artifacts, Advanced Blessing, Stella
3. This one fellow should represent 30-50% of your total power

### Roster Building
1. Get 15+ SSR/UR fellows to 3★ for the 4★ awakening gate
2. Assign matching-type fellows to buildings for earnings
3. Universal operators (Mammon, Adeline) fill any building type

### Why 120 Aptitude Matters Endgame
At Level 700 with heavy investment:
- 120 apt fellow: 120 x 13,680 = 1,641,600 base (before multipliers)
- 75 apt fellow: 75 x 13,680 = 1,026,000 base (before multipliers)
- The 120 apt fellow has **60% more base power**, and that gap WIDENS with every multiplier

### Archdemons Are Efficient
All 5 Archdemons have 75 aptitude and strong combat skills. They're cost-efficient for the 3★ gate because:
- Relatively common in SSR gacha
- Good awakening skills
- Useful in multiple game modes
