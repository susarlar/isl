# Museum — Building Guide

> **Note:** Public wiki data on the Museum is sparse. Most details below need in-game verification. Player input welcome.

## Overview

The Museum is a building in your village that houses cultural relics and trophies earned from events. It serves three main purposes:
1. **Gold earnings** over time (like other buildings)
2. **Hosts the Museum Minigame**
3. **Stores Museum Trophies and Exhibits** earned from events

### Fellow Operating Slots
The Museum supports up to **5 operating fellows**, with slots unlocking based on hired employees:
- Slot 1: Available on construction
- Slot 2: 50 employees hired
- Slot 3: 200 employees
- Slot 4: 800 employees
- Slot 5: 5,000 employees

**Best fellows for Museum:** Informed-type fellows benefit most. Cimitir is the specialty fellow.

## Museum Exhibits

Distinct from trophies, **Museum Exhibits** are individual items that can be earned through various event reward tracks. Confirmed example:

| Exhibit | Source | Rarity |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Gold Poker | Casino on Yacht (Premium Yacht Pass, 0 Points) | SSR |

Exhibits appear to be tied to gacha/event reward systems and are less common than trophies. The full exhibit list is not publicly documented.

## Museum Rank System

In the **May 2025 update**, the museum rank system received a usability upgrade:
- Easier to navigate
- "More satisfying to progress through" (per BlueStacks update notes)
- Aimed at completionists

**What "rank" means in the museum context** is not explicitly documented — likely tied to total trophies/exhibits collected or upgraded.

## Museum Minigame

The Museum building **hosts a Museum Minigame** accessible via an icon above the building. However:
- **No details about the minigame are publicly documented**
- Other minigames in the game include Maxim's Elixir Workshop (2048-style), Woof Woof Bakery (match-3), Fluffy Ranch
- Museum minigame mechanics, rewards, and frequency unknown

## Museum Tasks

Two **Fixed Titles** are unlocked through Museum Tasks:
- **Little Collector** — Unlocked through Museum Tasks
- **Elite Collector** — Unlocked through Museum Tasks

The exact requirements for these tasks are not documented but likely tied to collection milestones (number of trophies/exhibits owned or upgraded).

## Debt Ledger

The Museum's **Debt Ledger** has been mentioned in patch notes (skill descriptions were fixed in updates). What this is and how it functions is not documented. May be related to a specific exhibit or trophy with a debt-themed mechanic.

## Strategy Notes for Level 45+ Players

### Building Priority
- The Museum unlocks at Stage 153-6 (mid-game)
- As an Informed-type building, assign Informed fellows for max earnings
- Cimitir is the specialty fellow but generally low-tier — better Informed fellows include Augustine, Stephanie, Super, and any UR Informed (Neptune, Ao Li, Aegle)

### Trophy Collection
- **Participate in every mega event** that awards a trophy — they're permanent collection items
- Save event currency for trophy upgrades when available
- Some events may not be re-runnable, making missed trophies permanently unobtainable
