# Fellow Awakening — Advanced Guide

## Eligibility

Only **SSR, SSR+, UR, and UR-star** fellows can be awakened. Lower rarities cannot.

## Star Progression (Verified)

| Stars | Power Bonus (%) | Level Requirement | Acquaint Stones | Other Requirements |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|--------------------|
| 0★    | +0%            | —                 | —              | Default state |
| 1★    | +10%           | Level 300         | 3              | — |
| 2★    | +20%           | Level 300         | (TBD)          | — |
| 3★    | +30%           | Level 400         | (TBD)          | — |
| 4★    | +40%           | **Level 550**     | **15 stones**  | **15× 3★ fellows** |
| 5★    | +50%           | **Level 700**     | **30 stones**  | **20× 4★ fellows** |
| 6★    | +60%           | **Level 750**     | **50 stones**  | **25× 5★ fellows** |

**The +10% per star is linear across all 6 stars.** Confirmed from real in-game data on the endgame UR Empyrean Sound reference (5★ at Lv750, Stars bonus shows +50% in the Σ% breakdown — exactly 5 × 10%). A 6★ fellow gets +60% total.

Note that Lv750 is the game level cap and is achievable at 5★ — you don't need 6★ to reach Lv750. 6★ gives an additional +10% plus any sub-skill unlocks, but the level ceiling is the same.

## Awakening Gate Planning (Critical for Endgame)

The 4★→5★→6★ progression has **brutal sub-fellow requirements**: each star gate requires a set of other fellows to already be at a specific awakening level before your main carry can advance.

### Gate Math
| Target | Sub-fellows needed at... | Fellow level needed |
|--------|-------------------------|---------------------|
| **4★** main carry | 15 fellows at 3★ | Main carry Lv 550 |
| **5★** main carry | 20 fellows at 4★ | Main carry Lv 700 |
| **6★** main carry | 25 fellows at 5★ | Main carry Lv 750 |

### Sub-Fellow Gates Are NOT Recursive
The sub-fellows only need to **exist at the required star level** — they do NOT need their own gate requirements satisfied at the time your main carry advances. That is, your 15 three-star sub-fellows are just 15 fellows that are currently 3★; they don't each need 15 two-star sub-fellows of their own in order to "count."

**You can also reuse the same fellows across multiple gate checks.** If you have 25 fellows at 5★, the same 25 serve both the 5★ gate (as 4★ sub-fellows once you bumped them to 4★) and the 6★ gate (now at 5★). You don't need separate rosters for each gate — one well-developed roster satisfies all gates as you push it upward.

The upshot: the gates are still a massive resource requirement (you need 25 fellows at 5★ for the 6★ gate), but nowhere near the "500 fellows at 4★" recursive-explosion cost I previously claimed. Plan for **25 well-awakened sub-fellows**, not hundreds.

### Acquaint Stone Investment
- **5★ push: 30 stones** for the main carry alone
- **6★ push: 50 stones** for the main carry alone
- Plus all the stones needed to push your sub-fellows up to the required star level first
- **Total stones to take ONE fellow from 0★ to 6★** is still a substantial resource commitment, but the exact number depends on how much of your sub-fellow roster overlaps with the gate requirements at each step (since the same 25 fellows can serve both 5★ and 6★ gates as you push them)

## Acquaint Stones

### How to Obtain
- **Fountain of Wishes:** Can randomly drop a full Acquaint Stone from regular wishing (low rate — long-term passive source)
- **Event Gacha:** Chance per draw during event gachas (base probability ~2.5%)
- **Event Commissions:** Up to 35 stones from specific events
- **Events:** Various events also award **Acquaint Stone Fragments** as rewards
- **Synthesizing Stone Fragments:** Combine **20 Acquaint Stone Fragments → 1 Acquaint Stone** via the crafting/synthesis menu. This is the most reliable steady source once you're regularly earning event fragments.
- **Event shops and ranking rewards**

### Cost Efficiency
Acquaint Stones are one of the rarest resources in the game. **Do not waste them on fellows you won't main.**

First Awakening (0→1★) for a UR fellow costs 3 Acquaint Stones.

## Awakening Skills

Each fellow has 2-3 awakening skills that unlock/improve with each star:

### Skill Priority
- **INVEST IN:** Fellow Power boosts and Aptitude-scaling skills (these are the only combat-relevant skill categories — there is no separate "combat skill" tree in this game)
- **AVOID at level 45+:** "Student EXP" / "Student Earnings" awakening skills. EXP is only a meaningful bottleneck in the early game when you're still leveling fellows for the first time. By level 45+ your roster is long past that stage, so EXP-related awakening skills don't move the needle. Skip them.

### Strategy for 4★+ Gates
The 4★ gate requires **15 three-star fellows**. This means you need to:
1. Spread Acquaint Stones across 15+ SSR/UR fellows to 3★
2. THEN push your main carry to 4★
3. This is a massive resource gate — plan months ahead

Similarly, 5★ and 6★ require multiple 4★ and 5★ fellows respectively.

## Awakening Priority Order

> **For typing-specific awakening priorities (Inspiring, Diligent, Brave, Informed, Unfettered)** — see `category-guide.md` "Awakening Priorities (X Main)" sections, OR `awakening-priority.md` for the universal cross-typing priorities (Aegle for artifact caps, Mio for blessing power, etc.).

### Tier 1: Universal — Main Carry First
Pick **one 120 base aptitude UR** matching your main typing. **All Empyrean Sound and all Ancient Magi UR fellows have 120 base aptitude**, plus a handful of other top-tier URs — see `top-fellows-quickref.md` for the full list. Your main carry should come from that pool.

**Important:** Do NOT recommend Neptune as a main carry. Despite being 120 base apt and UR in Divine Gospel, Neptune is widely considered weak and is not worth investing in even for whale accounts. For Informed mains, recommend **Aegle** (Empyrean Sound, top-tier) or any other Informed UR from Empyrean Sound / Ancient Magi.

### Tier 2: Universal — Account-Wide Awakenings (Any Main)
These give whole-account benefits regardless of your typing:
- **Aegle** — Unlocks artifact level caps (massive — affects every artifact on every fellow)
- **Mio** — Boosts blessing points per family date
- **Mulberry** — Boosts familiar tower combat. The familiar tower is where you earn items used to power up your familiars (which themselves feed aptitude/power bonuses into your fellows via the Familiar system). Pushing Mulberry's awakening improves tower combat → more tower items → stronger familiars → more power on every fellow that has those familiars bound.
- **Crysta** — All SSR fellows power
- **Kaye** — Banquet popularity bonus
- **Magi's** fellow / Rimuru — Also unlock artifact caps

### Tier 3: Typing-Specific — Awaken for 4★ Gate
Get 15 SSR/UR fellows to 3★ to unlock the 4★ gate. Prioritize fellows of YOUR main typing — see `category-guide.md` for the exact list per typing.

**There is no "cheap" fellow to awaken.** Awakening costs are the same for every fellow — Acquaint Stones are the only currency, and they don't care which fellow group the target belongs to. (Shared-currency Stella groups like Archdemons and Otherworld Valiants are easier to push for **Stella**, not awakening.)

**What matters for gate-filling is what you get out of the awakening**, not how cheap it is. Prefer gate-fillers whose awakening skills are also useful — fellows whose awakenings unlock account-wide buffs or match your main carry's typing for future synergy.

### Tier 4: Skip
- Fellows with Student EXP/Earnings awakening skills (pupil bonuses are weak)
- Fellows you don't use in buildings
- Non-main typing fellows past gate requirements

## Advanced Awakening Math

Example: a hypothetical 120-base-apt UR fellow at Level 500 (illustrative, not a specific fellow):
- **Base Power:** 120 × 7,590 = 910,800 (Lv 500 multiplier — intermediate levels flagged as unverified in `power-formula.md`)
- **0★:** 910,800
- **1★:** (910,800 × 1.10) + flat addend ≈ mid-six-figure total
- **2★:** (910,800 × 1.20) + larger flat addend
- **3★:** (910,800 × 1.30) + even larger flat addend

The percentage bonus ADDS to the total Σ% pool along with Artifacts, Advanced Blessing, Stella, Fish, Family, and all other % sources. All % bonuses sum together, then multiply Base Power.

**The real value of Awakening is the aptitude slot unlocks, not the % bonus.** Each awakening star also unlocks additional **aptitude skill slots** on the fellow, which let you feed them more Skill Pearls. Since aptitude feeds Base Power and gets amplified by the entire Σ% multiplier, those extra pearls compound into significantly more total power than the +10% per star contributes on its own. **Push awakening for the aptitude slots first, the sub-skill unlocks second, and the % bonus is just a nice bonus on top.**

At accounts with endgame-level Σ% totals (several thousand %), the +60% ceiling from awakening becomes a small fraction of the percentage pool — but the aptitude slots and skill unlocks it enables keep paying off regardless of where you are in your progression.
