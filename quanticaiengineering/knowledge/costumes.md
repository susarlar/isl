# Costumes — Advanced Guide

## How Costumes Work

Costumes are **typed by one of the 5 fellow typings** (Brave, Diligent, Unfettered, Inspiring, Informed). The costume's bonuses target all fellows of that typing, plus the fellow wearing it.

**Critical rule: Costumes don't need to be equipped to provide buffs.** All owned costumes grant their bonuses passively. Collect everything.

**You upgrade a costume by acquiring the whole costume again** (i.e., pulling another copy). Each duplicate "upgrade" unlocks a new skill slot on the costume AND raises the level caps on its existing skills. In addition to the costume-level upgrades, you can level up the **individual skills** within each costume track by spending their respective resources (Skill Pearls or Fellow Costume Essences, depending on the skill).

## Two Costume Categories

1. **Fellow Costumes** → Aptitude and % power to the typing, plus personal buffs to the wearer
2. **Family Costumes** → Intimacy + Blessing Power, plus their own typing buffs that affect the family's blessed fellows

These are the **only two categories**. Nothing else.

## Fellow Costumes — By Rarity

Fellow costumes come in **2★, 3★, 4★, and 5★** rarity. 5★ fellow costumes are event-exclusive.

### 2★ Fellow Costume

Single skill:
- **Skill Pearl feed:** Fellow-only aptitude boost. Level cap **200**, **+6 aptitude per level** to the wearing fellow only.

That's it. 2★ costumes do nothing for the typing as a whole.

### 3★ Fellow Costume

Two skills:
- **Skill 1 — Skill Pearl feed:** Fellow-only aptitude. Level cap **200**, costs **2 Skill Pearls per level**, affects only the wearing fellow.
- **Skill 2 — Fellow Costume Essence feed:** Typing-wide aptitude. Level cap **30**, costs **15 Fellow Costume Essences per level**, gives **+1 aptitude per level to ALL fellows of the typing**.
- **Upgrading the costume (duplicate):** raises Skill 2's level cap by +30 each upgrade (so 30 → 60 → 90 → ...).

### 4★ Fellow Costume

Same structure as 3★ with one difference:
- **Skill 2 — Fellow Costume Essence feed:** Same level cap 30 / 15 essences per level, but gives **+2 aptitude per level** to all fellows of the typing (double the 3★ rate).
- Upgrades also raise the essence skill cap by +30 per duplicate.

### 5★ Fellow Costume (Event-Only)

Three skills — the top-tier fellow costume.

- **Skill 1 — Aptitude boost (Fellow Costume Essence feed):** **+30 aptitude per level**, costs 15 Fellow Costume Essences per level. Boosts the entire typing.
- **Skill 2 — Fellow Power %% boost:** Unlocks when you pull the **2nd copy** of the costume. Gives **+30% Fellow Power to the typing**. Upgraded with Fellow Costume Essences.
- **Skill 3 — Aptitude slot unlock:** Unlocks when you upgrade the costume a **3rd time**. Adds an extra aptitude slot to the fellow wearing it (they can be fed additional Skill Pearls into that slot).
- **Further costume upgrades** (4th copy and beyond): Each additional duplicate raises the level caps on all three skills, letting you push them further.

5★ fellow costumes are the single best per-costume investment in the game, but they're event-exclusive, so you can't farm them on demand — you get them when their event runs.

## Fellow Costume Essence Track

Fellow Costume Essences are the shared upgrade resource for all the "typing-wide aptitude" skill tracks on 3★/4★/5★ fellow costumes. Every costume you own of a given typing has its own essence track, and they all stack (no diminishing returns — see "Stacking" below).

### Essence Sources
- **Recurring events** drop Fellow Costume Essence chests as rewards
- **Costume Scrapbook** gives essence chests on completion milestones
- Limited events have given Fellow Costume Essences as flat rewards in the past, but these come and go; don't plan around any specific event

### Stacking
Multiple costumes of the same typing **all contribute simultaneously** — there is **no cap or diminishing returns** on how many costumes you stack. Every 3★/4★/5★ fellow costume you own of a typing adds its typing-wide aptitude independently.

## Family Costumes — By Rarity

Family costumes come in **2★, 3★, and 4★** tiers (no 5★ family costumes). They boost **Intimacy** and **Blessing Power** on the family, and some rarities add typing buffs and % power skills on top.

### 2★ Family Costume

Single activation bonus:
- **+100 Intimacy + +100 Blessing Power** on the family

No upgrade skills, no typing boosts. Lowest-tier.

### 3★ Family Costume

Activation bonus + multiple skill tracks that unlock as you upgrade the costume with duplicates:

- **Activation:** +300 Intimacy + +300 Blessing Power on the family
- **Each costume upgrade:** additional +500 Intimacy + +500 Blessing Power
- **Skill 1 (available at activation) — Typing aptitude boost:** Level cap **40**, costs **15 Family Costume Essences per level**, gives aptitude to all fellows of the costume's typing (max ~+40 aptitude at max level)
- **Skill 2 (unlocks at 2nd upgrade of the costume) — +5% Fellow Power to typing:** Level cap **4**, upgraded with **Family Costume Quintessence**
- **Skill 3 (unlocks at further costume upgrades) — +10% Fellow Power to typing:** another % power track that unlocks when the costume is pushed higher

### 4★ Family Costume

Highest-tier family costume. Same general structure as 3★ but with stronger numbers and additional skill unlocks:

- **Activation:** +500 Intimacy + +500 Blessing Power on the family
- **Each costume upgrade:** additional +1,000 Intimacy + +1,000 Blessing Power
- **Skill 1 "Heroically Gallant" (available at activation) — Typing aptitude:** Level cap **40**, costs **15 Family Costume Essences per level**, gives **+2 aptitude per level** to all fellows of the costume's typing
- **Skill 2 (unlocks at 2nd copy / upgrade 1):** Unlocks a **% Fellow Power boost on the typing**. Level cap **4**, upgraded with **Family Costume Quintessence**. Activation alone grants **+10% typing boost**, and the skill can be leveled up further.
- **Skill 3 (unlocks when costume is upgraded to level 3):** Aptitude blessing on the blessed fellow (a per-fellow aptitude bonus that applies to the specific fellow this family blesses)

**Family Costume Quintessence** is the separate upgrade resource for the % power skills on 3★/4★ family costumes. Quintessence is rarer than regular Family Costume Essence.

## Family Costume Essence Track
Family Costume Essences power the typing-aptitude skills on 3★ and 4★ family costumes, at **15 essences per level**. Sources are similar to Fellow Costume Essences: recurring events, event chests, and scrapbook completion rewards.

## How To Obtain Costumes

Costumes come from three main channels:

1. **Real-money purchases** — direct costume sales or premium bundles
2. **Daily task vouchers** — some costumes are locked behind accumulated voucher rewards from daily tasks
3. **Event rewards** — events drop costume **fragments** that synthesize into costumes once you collect enough
4. **Limited gacha banners** — costume-focused gacha pulls during events
5. **Costume Shop** — see below

### The Costume Shop (1st and 16th of Each Month)

The Costume Shop is open **twice a month, for 5 days each time**:
- **Opens on the 1st of the month**, runs for 5 days
- **Opens again on the 16th of the month**, runs for 5 days

Each shop window offers different costumes, though some costumes cycle back across multiple windows. **The shop is not infinite** — each costume has a finite fragment pool per window.

**Important:** Your fragment progress on a costume persists across shop windows. If you buy 43 fragments of a costume in one window and the costume needs 100 fragments total to craft, you still need **57 more fragments** in a future window to finish it. Buying 43 now doesn't "reset" — the missing 57 will be waiting for you when the costume comes back.

**Planning tip:** Figure out which costumes are in the current shop and whether you can afford to complete any this window. Don't spread voucher spending across many costumes if you'll lose progress between windows — concentrate on one or two you can finish.

## Costume Strategy for Level 45+ Players

### Priority 1: 5★ Fellow Costumes on Your Main Typing (When Events Run)
5★ costumes are the single strongest per-costume investment — three skills including a +30% Fellow Power track. When a 5★ event runs for your typing, **participate aggressively**. You cannot get these outside events.

### Priority 2: 4★ Fellow Costumes on Your Main Typing
Better typing-aptitude scaling (+2 per level vs +1 for 3★). Push these Essence tracks for every 4★ costume you own of your main typing.

### Priority 3: 4★ Family Costumes for Families That Bless Your Main Carry
Use `families-roster.md` to find which families bless your main carry. For each of those families, a 4★ family costume gives:
- +500 Intimacy + +500 Blessing Power at activation (direct Blessing Power is worth +2,260 BP/date after the 4.52 multiplier IF you date that family)
- +1,000 BP + +1,000 Intimacy per upgrade
- Typing aptitude via Heroically Gallant
- +10% typing power at activation (upgradable with Quintessence)
- Aptitude blessing on the blessed fellow at upgrade 3

### Priority 4: Max Out Every Essence Track You Own
Both Fellow Costume Essence tracks (on 3★/4★/5★ fellow costumes) and Family Costume Essence tracks (on 3★/4★ family costumes) should be pushed continuously. Stacking has **no diminishing returns** — more costumes of the same typing = more cumulative aptitude.

### Priority 5: Raise Caps With Duplicate Upgrades
Duplicate costumes are **never wasted**. Each duplicate raises level caps on existing skill tracks and unlocks new skills. Save them up; every copy is permanent account progression.

### Priority 6: 3★ Family Costumes As Fill-In
Lower priority than 4★ family costumes but still useful. +300 intimacy / +300 blessing at activation, typing aptitude, and up to +5%→+10% typing power through upgrade tracks.

### Priority 7: 2★ Fellow Costumes For Gate-Filler Main Carries
The Skill Pearl feed (level cap 200, +6 aptitude per level) is fellow-only but substantial if the costume sits on your actively-developed main carry. Low priority compared to the higher rarities.
