# Costumes — Advanced Guide

## How Costumes Work

Costumes are **typed by the fellow's aptitude category** (Inspiring, Diligent, Brave, Informed, Unfettered). A costume on an Inspiring fellow provides bonuses to ALL Inspiring fellows, not just the wearer.

**Critical rule: Costumes don't need to be equipped to provide buffs.** All owned costumes grant their bonuses passively. Collect everything.

## Two Costume Categories

1. **Fellow Costumes** → Aptitude boosts (typed by fellow's category)
2. **Family Costumes** → Intimacy + Blessing Power boosts

## Fellow Costumes — By Rarity

### 5-Star Costumes (Highest Value)

| Stage | Bonus | Scope | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| **Activation (0→1)** | +200 aptitude to the fellow | Fellow only | Massive personal boost |
| **Activation (0→1)** | +60 aptitude to ALL fellows of type | All of type | e.g., all Inspiring |
| **Level 2** | +30% Fellow Power of type | All of type | Multiplicative! |
| **Level 3** | Unlocks new aptitude slot (for skill pearls) | Fellow only | More pearl investment capacity |
| **Essence track** | +5 aptitude per step to ALL of type | All of type | 15 Fellow Costume Essences per step |
| **Essence cap** | 30 levels base (can raise with dupes) | All of type | 30→60→etc. with costume upgrades |

**5-star costumes give a 30% multiplicative power boost to an entire category.** This is huge — it compounds with Awakening, Stella, Artifacts, and Advanced Blessing.

## Fellow Costume Essence Track (All Rarities)

Every fellow costume (2★ through 5★) has a **Fellow Costume Essence upgrade track**:

- **Cost:** 15 Fellow Costume Essences per level
- **Bonus:** +1 aptitude to ALL fellows of that type per level (3★ and below), +5 per step (5★)
- **Base cap:** 30 levels
- **Cap increase:** Upgrading the costume with duplicates raises cap: 30 → 60 → higher
- **Stacks across costumes:** Multiple costumes on the same type all contribute

### Essence Sources
- "From Old Country Bumpkin to Master Swordsman in Isekai" event: 400 units
- "Hanamiya Rica Special" event: 210 units
- Other limited events

### Why the Essence Track Matters
Even a 2★ costume provides +1 aptitude to ALL fellows of a type per 15 essences. If you have 10 Inspiring costumes all with maxed essence tracks at cap 30, that's +300 aptitude to ALL Inspiring fellows. This stacks significantly.

## Costume Upgrade (Duplicates)

Getting duplicate costumes allows you to **upgrade** the original, which:
1. Raises the essence track cap (30 → 60 → higher)
2. Increases the maximum aptitude contribution from that costume

This means "bad" gacha duplicate costumes are never wasted — they increase the cap.

## Family Costumes

Family costumes provide **Intimacy + Blessing Power**, NOT aptitude.

| Rarity | Intimacy Bonus | Blessing Power Bonus |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|
| 2-star | +100 | +100 |
| 3-star | +300 | +300 |

Since Blessing Points = Blessing Power × 4.52 per date, a 3-star family costume (+300 BP) adds ~1,356 extra Blessing Points per non-CG date, or ~20K extra per day across 15 dates.

## Costume Strategy for Level 45+ Players

### Priority 1: 5-Star Costumes on Your Main Type
The +30% Fellow Power is multiplicative and category-wide. If you main Inspiring and get a 5-star Inspiring costume, it boosts your ENTIRE Inspiring roster by 30%. This compounds with:
- Awakening stars
- Stella category boost
- Artifacts
- Advanced Blessing
- Fish % bonuses

### Priority 2: 4-Star Costumes on Your Main Type
+10% Fellow Power category-wide is still a meaningful multiplier.

### Priority 3: Maximize Essence Tracks
Every costume you own contributes to the essence track for its type. Max these out — the cumulative aptitude boost to all fellows of a type adds up fast.

### Priority 4: Raise Caps with Duplicates
Don't discard duplicate costumes. Use them to upgrade and raise the essence track cap from 30 → 60.

### Priority 5: 3-Star Family Costumes
+300 Blessing Power per costume directly increases your daily Blessing Point income by ~20K/day.
