# Awakening Priority — Guild Guide

> Source: Guild guide. Which fellows to awaken first and why, especially in the 0★ → 4★ range.

## The "Up to 4★" Priority List

These fellows should be awakened first because their awakening unlocks valuable game-wide bonuses.

### 1. Artifact Levels (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

**Awakening these fellows raises the level cap on ALL artifacts:**

- **Aegle** (UR Informed)
- **Magi's** fellow (specific Magi fellow whose awakening boosts artifact caps)
- **Rimuru Tempest** (Slime collab)
- (And similar — any fellow whose awakening grants "Artifact Level limit +X")

**Example: Aegle 4★** unlocks:
- Level limit for ALL artifacts +18 (next level +20)
- Negotiation power
- Expo sales ability
- Archdemon challenge power of all Informed fellows +27% (next +30%)
- All Informed fellow power +7% (next +8%)

This is why Aegle is the F2P-friendly Informed main — even if you don't main Informed, awakening Aegle to 4★ benefits your whole account.

### 2. Blessing Power Boost

Awakening these fellows boosts blessing point gain (compounding with the BP × 4.52 formula):

- **Mio** (4★ awakening: each date with a family member gives that member +9% more blessing points)
- **Emosen**
- **Lux**
- **Shlomo**

If you can max these to 4★, every family date you do gives more BP for the rest of your account's life.

### 3. Quality of Life (Non-Essential)

These give nice but not critical bonuses:

| Fellow | What Awakening Boosts |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Mulberry** | Familiar tower combat power |
| **Crysta** | All SSR fellow power |
| **Kaye** | Banquet popularity bonus |

### 4. Event-Dependent

Only push these if you actively participate in their respective events:

| Event | Fellows to Awaken |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Touno (Island Story)** | Iori, Amaterasu |
| **Tree (World Tree Cup)** | Tigirl, Flos, Onikiri, Orivita |
| **Rimuru main/semi-main** | Milim Nava, Benimaru |

### 5. Conditional (Skip Pearl Slot Unlocks Until Invested)

**Leon, Kerr & Bel & Ros**, and similar high-rarity fellows — only awaken these if they're already strong enough to benefit from MORE skill pearl slots. Awakening a fellow you haven't invested in doesn't give you anywhere near the value of awakening a fellow you're actively building, because the extra skill pearl slots are only useful if you have pearls to put in them.

**Do NOT prioritize Neptune** — despite being 120 apt UR, Neptune is widely considered weak and not recommended as a main carry even for whales.

## What's Next After 4★

Once your priority fellows hit 4★, **start upgrading your main typing for extra skill pearl slots** (each awakening star unlocks more slots, which means more aptitude investment ceiling — this is the real value of awakening at endgame, not the +10% per star).

## Things to AVOID Awakening

**Anything that boosts PUPILS is awful** — avoid at all costs UNLESS the fellow is one of your main typing's SSRs.

Pupil-related awakening rewards (like "+earnings to graduated students") are weak compared to direct power scaling.

## Awakening Gates (Verified)

Remember the level + acquaint stone requirements:

| Gate | Fellow Level | Stones | Sub-Fellows Required |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| 4★ | Lv 550 | 15 | 15× 3★ fellows |
| 5★ | Lv 700 | 30 | 20× 4★ fellows |
| 6★ | Lv 750 | 50 | 25× 5★ fellows |

Sub-fellow gates are **NOT recursive** — sub-fellows only need to exist at the required star level, they don't need their own gates satisfied. The **same 25 fellows** serve both the 5★ and 6★ gates as you push them from 4★ → 5★ → 6★ over time. Plan for one well-developed roster of ~25 sub-fellows that you progress alongside your main carry.

## Why This Order

The priority list is ordered by **scope of benefit**:
1. **Artifact Level Cap** (Aegle) — affects EVERY equipped artifact on EVERY fellow
2. **Blessing Power** (Mio etc.) — affects EVERY family date you'll ever do
3. **QoL** — affects specific systems (familiars, SSR pool, banquets)
4. **Event-specific** — affects one event meta
5. **Skill Pearl slots** — affects only the fellow being awakened

The earlier in the list, the broader the benefit per acquaint stone spent.
