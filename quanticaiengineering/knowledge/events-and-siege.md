# Events & Siege Warfare — Guild Guide

## Siege Warfare Strategy

Siege Warfare is a guild event where you attack enemy bases with team compositions. The key mechanic affects how many fellows you should bring per team.

### The Team Loss Mechanic

**Every time your team wins a duel within a match chain, the team loses 1% of total team power** (only during that match chain — recovers between match chains).

This means **bigger teams = more total power lost per win**. You want minimum-fellow teams to minimize the loss.

### Optimal Team Sizes

| Base Tier | Team Size | Reasoning |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Low-tier bases** | **1 fellow** | Single carry can clear, minimal % loss |
| **Mid-tier bases** | **2 fellows** | Need backup, but keep losses small |
| **High-tier bases** | **3 fellows** | Tougher bases require more bodies, accept some loss |

**Never use full teams** — the 1% per win compounds and you lose massive power on long match chains.

### Same Typing Bonus

Fellows of the **same typing in a team get a minor boost** to each other. Combined with the small team rule:
- 1-fellow team: trivially "same typing" (just the one)
- 2-fellow team: pair fellows of the same typing for the buff
- 3-fellow team: try to keep all 3 on the same typing

### Why Minimum Teams Win
- Small teams = less total power → smaller absolute power loss per duel
- Same typing buff still applies
- Faster to set up multiple teams across multiple bases
- Allows you to attack more bases in a session

## Best Events to Whale On

1. **Cloud Kingdom Games (Olympics)** — Direct Stella purchase for Heracles, **best loot in any event**
2. **Penglai Immortal** — Master Tongxuan Stella + very good rewards
3. **Casino on Yacht** — Common, easy UR piece farming, mid loot
4. **Frantic Forest** — Easy to farm even at low spend

## Worst Events to Invest In

1. **Sandtopia** — Leon is dead, very rare event
2. **Guild Trade Festival** — Kerr has no outfit, very rare event
3. **World Tree Cup** — Bad rewards (unless using alt accounts)
4. **Northern Odyssey** — No Sunna Stella until you get her outfit

## Key F2P Plays

- **Aegle Stella 10 (Free)** — Daily gacha ticket gives you points → trade for Aegle fragments. Get to Stella 10 free over time.
- **Orivita Stella 10 (Alt accounts)** — Make alt accounts for Tree event, feed fragments to main. Also unlimited Orivita outfit upgrades this way.
- **Aloha cheap path** — Nierus Stella is fairly cheap to get even at low spend.
