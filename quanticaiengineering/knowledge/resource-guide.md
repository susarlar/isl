| Source | Amount | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Fountain of Wishes | 1 per 500 wishes | Long-term |
| Event Gacha | 2.5% per draw | Per event |

| Crafting (20 Fragments → 1) | Variable | Ongoing |
