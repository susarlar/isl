# Blessing & Intimacy Strategy — Guild Guide

> Source: Guild guide. Verified with the BP = Blessing Power × 4.52 formula.

## Intimacy

### The Core Rule
**Bonds are mostly always a scam.** The only one that's kind of worth it is the bond that increases blessing points. Otherwise, **the earnings boost from intimacy far outscales any of the typing % bonds**.

**Family rarity does NOT matter for intimacy** — they all give the same earnings.

### Intimacy Investment Order (Optimal)

Build ALL your family members through these stages, doing each stage to ALL families before moving to the next:

| Stage | Target | How to Do It |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | 2,000 each | Build all family to 2,000 first |
| 2 | 5,000 each | Boost 500 at a time across families (or 5,000 each at once) |
| 3 | 8,000 each | Same approach |
| 4 | 10,000 each | Same |
| 5 | 12,000 each | Same |
| 6 | 15,000 each | Same |
| 7 | 20,000 each | Same |
| 8+ | Higher | Continue past 20k |

**Optimal pattern:** 500 at a time across all families (slightly better than maxing one family before moving to the next, but doing 5,000 each is OK too — it's the same final goal).

### Why Bonds Are A Scam
The "+X% to Y typing fellows" bond rewards seem appealing but are tiny compared to the earnings boost from raw intimacy. Skip them.

The only exception: **the bond that increases Blessing Points earned per date** is worth pursuing because it directly compounds with the Blessing Power × 4.52 formula.

## Blessing Power

### The Core Insight
**The more Blessing Power a family character has, the more Blessing Points each date gives** (per the verified formula: `Blessing Points = Blessing Power × 4.52` for non-CG dates, `× 5.42` for CG dates).

This means leveling a family's Blessing Power compounds — every +1 Blessing Power = +4.52 Blessing Points per date forever after.

### Finding Your Investment Targets

1. Sort your fellows by power (filtered to your main typing)
2. Check the family lists of your top main typing fellows
3. Look for **families that appear on more than one of your main characters** — they give the most power per blessing investment
4. These are your priority blessing targets

### Blessing Investment Order

| Stage | Target | Apply To |
|-------|--------|----------|
| 1 | All shared families to 20k | Families that bless 2+ of your main typing fellows |
| 2 | Main carry's family to 50k | The single best family |
| 3 | Main carry's family to 100k | If you have the resources |

**Don't waste blessing on:**
- Family members that don't boost your main typing (e.g., putting 20k blessing into Gina if you main Inspiring)
- 1x blessing tier families
- Families that only bless fellows you don't use

### Compounding With Family Stella
Every +1 Blessing Power earned by leveling a family member becomes more valuable when combined with maxing that family member's Stella, because Family Stella adds +800 blessing power directly per UR Stella maxed (which then earns +800 × 4.52 = +3,616 BP per date forever).

## The Combined Strategy

1. **Intimacy:** All families to 2,000 → 5,000 → 8,000 → ... → 20,000+ (evenly across all families)
2. **Blessing:** Identify shared families on your main typing → 20k blessing each → 50k → 100k on your top family

Do these in parallel — intimacy is for economy (gold), blessing is for power. Both compound massively but serve different purposes.

## Quick Math Example

Family at 20,000 Blessing Power:
- Non-CG date: 20,000 × 4.52 = **90,400 BP per date**
- CG date: 20,000 × 5.42 = **108,400 BP per date**
- 15 daily dates @ ~40% CG rate: **~1.45M BP per day**

Family at 100,000 Blessing Power:
- Non-CG: 452,000 BP per date
- CG: 542,000 BP per date  
- 15 daily dates: **~7.27M BP per day** (5x more income)

This is why pushing your main family to 100k is worth it once your shared families are at 20k.
