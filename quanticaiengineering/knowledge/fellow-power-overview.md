# Fellow Power Optimization — Overview

> **Critical:** For the complete verified power formula derived from real in-game data, see `power-formula.md`. That document supersedes any older math claims.

## Core Formula (Verified)

```
Total Power = (Base × (1 + Σ%) + ΣFlat) × (1 + FinalMultiplier%)
Base = Total Aptitude × Level Multiplier
```

## Key Principle: All Percentage Bonuses Are ADDITIVE

Unlike many gacha games, Isekai: Slow Life does **NOT compound percentage multipliers**. They all add together into a single sum, which is then multiplied with Base Power.

### Real Example (Amaterasu, verified)
Amaterasu has these percentage bonuses:
- Stars: +50%
- Family: +2,019.5%
- Artifacts: +788.25%
- Skill: +501%
- Fish: +2,624.5%
- Museum: +519.7%
- Familiar: +310%
- Compendium: +280%
- Figure: +140%
- Demon's Blessing: +706%
- Building Training: +125%
- Expo: +25.2%
- Resonance Power: +10%
- Building Appearance: +0%

**Total = +8,099.15%** (literally summed)

Her final power is `Base × 81.99 + Flat × Final`, which equals her in-game value of 48.47B within 0.014% accuracy.

## Why Aptitude Is King

Because Base Power = Aptitude × Level Multiplier, and Base gets multiplied by the huge percentage sum (8,000%+ for endgame fellows), **every single aptitude point is worth thousands of total power**.

### Example
For Amaterasu:
- Each +1 aptitude point = +15,500 base (Lv 750 multiplier)
- × 81.99 (her percentage sum) = **+1.27M total power per aptitude point**
- × 1.14 final = **+1.45M total power per aptitude point**

This is why:
- Skill Pearls (aptitude) matter enormously
- Costume essence tracks matter
- Fish aptitude skills matter
- Family Stella aptitude contributions matter
- Every tiny aptitude source compounds through the formula

## Strategic Insights from the Verified Formula

### Insight 1: Aptitude Investment Compounds Through the %
A +10 aptitude from a costume is actually worth: 10 × 15,500 × 82 = **12.7M total power on Amaterasu**. That "tiny" +10 is massive when multiplied through.

### Insight 2: Early-Game % Bonuses Matter More (Relatively)
At 500% total bonus, adding +30% from Awakening is a +6% relative gain. At 8,000% total bonus, adding +30% is a +0.4% relative gain.

**This means:** Focus on % bonuses EARLY. Focus on aptitude LATE.

### Insight 3: Single Carry Meta Still Works (Different Reason)
Stacking bonuses on one fellow still gives a 5–10x advantage over spreading, but not for the reason I originally stated. It works because:
- You get to sum ALL % bonuses on one fellow
- You invest ALL aptitude sources on one fellow
- Both feed the same formula

### Insight 4: "Item" and "Building Service Level" Are Hidden Giants
Amaterasu has +467M from "Item" flat bonus and +157M from "Building Service Level". These aren't documented in any strategy guide but are major contributors.

### Insight 5: Resonance Power Double-Counting
Resonance Power appears in BOTH the regular % sum AND the Final Multiplier. This means it's applied twice — once as part of the sum, once as a final multiplier. This is the only source that double-dips.

## Common Misconceptions (Corrected)

❌ "Multipliers compound exponentially" → **All % bonuses are additive**  
❌ "Addends are worthless late game" → **Flat bonuses still add up (3–5% of total)**  
❌ "Awakening is the most important multiplier" → **Family Stella and Fish are way bigger sources**  
❌ "Spreading bonuses gives compound returns" → **Both spreading and stacking are additive; stacking wins by volume**  
❌ "Brave/Unfettered are permanently behind due to no Category Stella" → **They lose ~30% of additive pool, which is a ~0.4% loss at endgame (not fatal)**
