# Fellow Power Calculation — Advanced Guide

## Core Formula

**Base Power = Aptitude x Level Multiplier**

The Level Multiplier scales non-linearly with fellow level:

| Level | Power per Aptitude Point |
|-------|-------------------------|
| 1     | 300                     |
| 100   | 925                     |
| 200   | 1,927                   |
| 300   | 3,362                   |
| 400   | 5,230                   |
| 500   | 7,590                   |
| 600   | 10,442                  |
| 700   | 13,680                  |

## Addends vs Multipliers — The Critical Distinction

This is the single most important concept for Fellow Power optimization. The game's math has two types of bonuses:

### Additive Bonuses (Addends)
These add flat amounts to power:
- Fellow Leveling (increases base power linearly)
- Fellow Blessing from Family (flat power: +1K to +112K+)
- Awakening flat bonus component (+400K at 1-star, +800K at 2-star, +1.5M at 3-star)

### Multiplicative Bonuses (Multipliers)
These compound with each other exponentially:
- Limit Break bonuses
- Artifacts
- Advanced Blessing from Family (percentage: +0.5% to +12.5%+)
- Awakening percentage component (+10% per star)
- Stella category-wide buffs
- Category/Group synergy bonuses

### Why This Matters

**Multipliers compound together.** If you have 3 separate +20% multipliers on one fellow:
- Total = Base x 1.2 x 1.2 x 1.2 = Base x 1.728 (72.8% boost)
- NOT Base x 1.6 (60% boost if additive)

**The optimal strategy is to stack ALL multipliers onto your single main carry fellow.** Spread additive bonuses (leveling, flat blessings) across remaining fellows.

## Total Power Components (All Sources)

1. **Base Power** — Aptitude x Level Multiplier
2. **Awakening** — Both % and flat bonus per star
3. **Artifacts** — Multiplier (must be reforged to activate)
4. **Costume Aptitude** — Increases Aptitude stat, feeds into base power
5. **Skill Pearl Aptitude** — Increases Aptitude stat, feeds into base power
6. **Family Blessings** — Fellow Blessing (flat) + Advanced Blessing (%)
7. **Stella** — Character-specific + category-wide power boosts
8. **Fish Tank** — Power and aptitude boosts from placed fish + combo bonuses
9. **Category/Group Synergy** — Same-category fellows boost each other

## Optimization Priority for Advanced Players

1. **Awakening stars on your main** — Each star is +10% multiplicative
2. **Stella investment** — Category-wide multiplier affects your entire roster
3. **Artifacts with matching types** — Multiplicative, must match fellow aptitude type
4. **Advanced Blessing levels** — Percentage-based, scales better than Fellow Blessing late-game
5. **Skill Pearls on Supreme Talent** — Highest aptitude ceiling (900 at cap)
6. **Costume collection** — Passive aptitude even when not equipped
7. **Fish combinations** — Permanent power boosts, often overlooked
8. **Level grinding** — Important but additive; diminishing returns compared to multiplier stacking
