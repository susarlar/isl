# Families Roster — Blessings & Stella Rarities

> Complete list of families, their rarities, and which fellows they bless. Data collected in-game by the reference account.
>
> **Family rarity = Family Stella rarity.** UR families have UR Family Stella, SSR families have SSR Family Stella, etc. See `family-stella.md` for the mechanical differences between UR and SSR Family Stella tables.
>
> **"(with fellow stella)" / "(based on fellow stella)"** next to a fellow name means that pairing is not available by default — you must advance the relevant Fellow Stella first to unlock the blessing.

## 🎯 How To Use This Doc

When a player asks "which family should I push?" or "how do I boost my [fellow]?", do this:
1. Find the fellow's name in the roster below (search by Ctrl-F or by scanning)
2. Collect every family that blesses them, grouped by rarity
3. Recommend maxing the UR families first, then the SSR families, etc.
4. If the pairing has "(with fellow stella)", warn the user they need to advance that fellow's group Stella before the family blessing unlocks

## Key Observations

### Amaterasu Gets Massive Family Support
Amaterasu is blessed by multiple UR and SSR families (Phantanyl, Tsukuyomi, Thubran, Kagura, Futaba, Bridget, Sera, and more). This is one reason she's such a dominant Inspiring main carry — you can stack many Family Stellas on her simultaneously.

### Some Fellows Barely Get Blessings
Certain fellows appear in very few family rosters. For example, pure-SR or R fellows are mostly blessed only by SR/R families. If you're thinking of a niche fellow as a main carry, check this roster first — if they only appear in 1-2 SR/R family pairings, their Family Stella ceiling is low compared to a top UR.

### Fellow-Stella-Gated Pairings
Many family pairings carry a "(with fellow stella)" or "(based on fellow stella)" flag, meaning they unlock only after you push the relevant Fellow Stella. If you're planning around a specific family blessing, verify whether it's available now or if you need to progress Fellow Stella first.

### Lancelot Is Uniquely Flexible
Lancelot has fixed pairings with Magellan and Jewlry but then gives additional **Stella-based fellow choices** as the Family Stella advances — meaning you can pick which fellows Lancelot blesses over time. This is unusual among families; most have fixed blessing lists.

### Upgrade Paths (SSR → UR Families)
A few SSR families can be upgraded to UR: **Lancelot**, **Hestia**, **Ixtchel**. Upgrading them converts their Family Stella from SSR tables to UR tables, which is a large upgrade (~4x stronger).

## Data Completeness

This roster was collected in-game by one account. Known limitations:
- Typings in parentheses come from observational labels; some may be slightly mis-tagged
- Some families may have additional blessings that only unlock at higher Family Stella levels than the reference account has reached
- New families added in future updates aren't here yet
