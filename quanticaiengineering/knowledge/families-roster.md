# Families Roster — Blessings, Rarities & Slot Mechanics

> Complete list of families, their rarities, blessed fellows, and the mechanics of how family blessings work. Data collected in-game by the reference account.
>
> **Family rarity = Family Stella rarity.** UR families have UR Family Stella, SSR families have SSR Family Stella, etc. See `family-stella.md` for the mechanical differences between rarity tiers.

---

## How Blessings Work

Each family blesses a specific list of fellows. There are **three distinct slot types** to understand:

### 1. Fixed Slots
The family always blesses these specific fellows by default. No way to change them.

### 2. Fellow Stella-Based Slots (Conditional)
The slot is already assigned to a specific fellow, but only **activates** when you level that FELLOW's own Stella to a certain point.

**Example:** "Hermes (based on fellow stella)" in Denier's list means Hermes is the fixed assignee of that slot, but Denier's blessing on Hermes only kicks in once you push Hermes's own Fellow Stella high enough.

Marked in the roster below as **"(based on fellow stella)"** or **"(with fellow stella)"** next to the fellow name.

### 3. Family Stella-Based Custom Slots ⭐ (Most Flexible)
The family has **empty slots** that YOU choose which fellow to fill in, unlocked by leveling the FAMILY'S own Stella. These are the most valuable slots in the game because you can assign them to your main carry regardless of typing.

**Constraint:** Different custom slots on the same family must hold **different fellows** — you cannot put the same fellow into multiple custom slots of one family.

**Families with Custom Slots:**
- **Lancelot** (SSR → UR upgradable) — Fixed slots on Magellan and Jewlry (both fellows), plus custom slots that unlock as Lancelot's Family Stella advances
- **Hestia** (SSR → UR upgradable) — Has Fixed slots (Gabrael, Aegle, Ais Wallenstein, Bell Cranel) plus custom slots unlocked via Family Stella
- **Hanamiya Rica** (SSR → UR upgradable, **limited event family**) — Has custom slots that unlock as her Family Stella advances. Rica was a streamer collab / limited event character, so she's not always obtainable.

### Custom Slot Strategy
Custom-slot families are the **only way Brave and Unfettered mains can partially compensate** for the missing SR Category Stella (Rani/Elise/Liz/Angie don't exist for those typings). You can stack Lancelot + Hestia + Hanamiya Rica custom slots all onto the same main carry for a typing-agnostic multiplier stack.

However, remember the constraint: each custom slot must hold a **different** fellow, so within a single family you can't quadruple-dip on one carry. You'd stack them across multiple custom-slot families.

## 🎯 How To Use This Doc

When a player asks "which family should I push?" or "how do I boost my [fellow]?":
1. Find the fellow's name in the roster below
2. Collect every family that blesses them, grouped by rarity
3. Recommend maxing the UR families first, then the SSR families, etc.
4. If a pairing has "(with fellow stella)" or "(based on fellow stella)", warn the user they need to advance that fellow's Fellow Stella before the blessing activates
5. For any main carry that has custom-slot-families available (Lancelot, Hestia, Hanamiya Rica), mention those as a stackable typing-agnostic bonus

### What This Means Strategically
- **Amaterasu, Sunna, and Freesia** are tied at 7 families each with mostly fixed blessings. Amaterasu and Sunna are Inspiring/Diligent, Freesia is Brave.
- **Freesia's family count includes 3 UR families** (Curren, Mors, Wadjetta) — the highest UR-family density in the game. Combined with Frantic Forest running reasonably often, Freesia is the strongest practical Brave main for new accounts.
- **Leon and Heracles tied at 6 families each** for Brave. Choose based on event access and which families you already have unlocked.
- **Master Tongxuan has 6 families but 2 are fellow-stella-gated** (Bathery and Athena only unlock once Master Tongxuan's own Fellow Stella reaches Lv 4). Effective early-game count is 4, growing to 6.
- **Hermes has 6 families with 2 fellow-stella-gated** (Denier and Mushimi unlock at Hermes's Fellow Stella Lv 4+). Effective early-game count is 4, growing to 6.
- **Aegle has 6 families including 2 UR** (Curren and Namiko) plus the Hestia custom slot, making her the strongest Informed main.

**The "family count" above is a rough proxy, not a power ranking.** What actually matters is how much effort you can put into acquiring those families and pushing their Family Stella. A 4-family fellow with all 4 families maxed beats a 7-family fellow where you've only acquired 2 of the 7.

## Key Observations

### Custom Slot Families Are The Keystone For Non-Category-Stella Typings
Brave and Unfettered have no standalone SR Category Stella (Rani/Elise are Inspiring, Liz is Diligent, Angie is Informed). The only way to partially close that ~30% multiplier gap is through **custom slot families (Lancelot, Hestia, Hanamiya Rica)**, where you can assign any fellow to the unlocked slot. These three families are disproportionately important for Brave and Unfettered mains.

### Dragon Maid Collab: Emma vs Elma
- **Elma** (Dragon Maid SSR, Unfettered) — separate fellow
- **Emma the Ork** (Tsukimichi SSR, Diligent) — separate fellow, blessed by Raphael
- These are **two different fellows** despite the similar names. Do not conflate them.

### Greyrat Family (Mushoku Tensei Collab)
**Sylphiette Greyrat is a FAMILY MEMBER, not a fellow.** Families don't have typings. She's the head of the Greyrat Family in-game and her Family Stella blesses: Nemetona, Roxy Migurdia, Aisha Greyrat, Rudeus Greyrat, and Beryl.

The separate **Greyrat Family Stella Group** (for Fellow Stella purposes) consists of the **fellows**: Rudeus Greyrat (Brave), Aisha Greyrat (Inspiring), Roxy Migurdia (Informed). Don't confuse the family head (Sylphiette) with the Fellow Stella group (which is a different system).

### Fellows With Heavy Family Support
- **Amaterasu and Sunna** — 7 families each, with mostly fixed pairings (no Fellow-Stella gating). The strongest Inspiring and Diligent carries by family count.
- **Leon, Heracles, Hermes, Master Tongxuan, Aegle, Nemetona** — 6 families each. All competitive as main carries assuming you've acquired the families.
- **Aegle's 6 families include 2 UR** (Curren, Namiko) plus the Hestia custom slot — one of the strongest Informed lineups available.

### Fellow Stella Gating Affects Practical Family Count
Several fellows have fellow-stella-gated blessings (meaning the blessing only unlocks once you push the fellow's Fellow Stella to a certain level, typically Lv 4). For **Master Tongxuan**, 2 of his 6 families (Bathery and Athena) are gated. For **Hermes**, 2 of her 6 families (Denier and Mushimi) are gated. This means in the early game these fellows have effectively 4 active families each — you earn the other 2 as their Fellow Stella advances.

### Always Check Acquisition Before Recommending
A fellow being "blessed by 6 families" is only valuable if the player actually has those families unlocked. Event-gated families and collab families may not be accessible to every player, especially newer accounts. Always assume the player may not have every family when making Family Stella push recommendations.

### Data Completeness
This roster was collected in-game by one account. Known limitations:
- Some typings in parentheses are observational labels and may be slightly mis-tagged
- Some families may have additional blessings that only unlock at higher Family Stella levels than the reference account has reached
- New families added in future updates aren't here yet
- Limited-event families (like Hanamiya Rica) may be unavailable to most accounts

If you notice a missing family or wrong pairing, contact **Su** to get it updated.
