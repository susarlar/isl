# Pearls — Complete Reference

The game has **three distinct pearl currencies**, each with its own shop, purpose, and power contribution. They do NOT stack or convert into each other. Do not confuse them.

## Quick Comparison

| Pearl | Primary Use | Where It Applies |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| **Skill Pearls** (gold-tier) | Level up Talent aptitude skills on fellows | Fellow aptitude only — NOT buildings |
| **White Pearls** | Improve **bait production speed** | Fishing — bait economy |
| **Black Pearls** | Buy **account-wide % boosts** from the Black Pearl shop | Multiple power-adjacent systems |

## Skill Pearls (Gold Tier)

**Full reference:** see `skill-pearls.md`.

TL;DR — Skill Pearls are spent to level up Talent skills (Supreme / Outstanding / Ordinary) on individual fellows. Each pearl gives +1 aptitude regardless of tier; tier determines the cap (900 / 600 / 300). **Skill Pearls are fellow-only — you cannot spend them on buildings, fish, artifacts, or any other system.**

## White Pearls

White Pearls are obtained from the **shop** (regular village shop, not event shops) and are spent exclusively on improving **bait production speed** in your fishing/bait buildings.

### What they do
- Permanently raise how fast your bait production buildings generate Normal Bait per tick
- Stack across multiple purchase levels — keep buying as long as you have pearls
- **This is the main lever to increase your fish-catch throughput**, which multiplies the effect of every fish skill you've unlocked

### Why they matter
Fishing is the #1 source of power percentage in the game (+2,624% on endgame Amaterasu). More bait → more catches → faster fish skill levels → more Σ% on every fellow. White Pearls are an indirect but very high-leverage power source.

### Strategy
- Buy White Pearls whenever they're in the shop
- Always dump them into bait production speed
- Do this before chasing the shop's "fancier" offers — baseline bait throughput is foundational

## Black Pearls

Black Pearls are a higher-tier currency spent in a dedicated **Black Pearl shop** that sells **permanent account-wide % boosts**. Unlike White Pearls (single-purpose bait speed), Black Pearls buy several different upgrade tracks.

### Upgrade Tracks Available
| Track | What It Boosts |
|-------|----------------|
| **Aptitude** | Fellow aptitude — feeds directly into Base Power |
| **Fellow Power %** | Adds to the Σ% pool on fellows |
| **Village Earnings** | Gold income from your buildings |
| **Family Intimacy** | Intimacy gain rate when dating family members (speeds up Advanced Blessing progression) |
| **Family Blessing** | Direct blessing point output per date |

### Why they matter
The Aptitude and Fellow Power % tracks contribute directly to the power formula — they sit in the same buckets as skill pearls / artifacts / fish and get amplified by everything downstream. The Village Earnings track accelerates every Gold-gated system (buildings, employees, blueprints). The Family Intimacy and Family Blessing tracks accelerate the largest Σ% source after Fish.

### Priority Order for Black Pearls
For advanced players pushing Fellow Power:
1. **Aptitude track** — compounds with the highest multiplier stack, best long-term ROI
2. **Fellow Power %** — direct Σ% contribution
3. **Family Intimacy / Blessing** — if you're not yet at max blessing bonds on your main carry
4. **Village Earnings** — only push this if your Gold income is the bottleneck on buildings/blueprints

Don't split pearls evenly — focus on one track at a time until its next tier becomes uneconomical, then move on.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Treating "pearls" as one currency.** They're three separate systems with different shops and uses. Check which pearl you're holding before spending.
2. **Spending White Pearls on anything other than bait speed.** There's nothing else to spend them on — the shop only offers bait production upgrades.
3. **Thinking Skill Pearls can go into buildings.** They cannot. Skill Pearls are fellow-aptitude-only.
4. **Black Pearls on Village Earnings when already Gold-rich.** Earnings is the lowest-impact Black Pearl track if your Gold income already covers your building/blueprint spend.
