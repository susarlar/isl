# Family → Fellow Blessing Chart

## How Blessing Tiers Work

Each Family member has a fixed list of Fellows they can bless. The blessing tier (1x–4x) determines how many fellows that family member boosts. **UR+ Family members can choose which fellows to bless** (flexible slots).

Higher-tier families bless more fellows, but the blessing strength (Fellow Blessing / Advanced Blessing levels) is the same regardless of tier — it's the number of fellows covered that changes.

## Flexible Blessing Slots (UR+ and Stella-Based)

UR+ Family members can **choose** which fellows to bless. Additionally, some family members (Lancelot, Magellan, Jewlry) unlock flexible slots through their **Stella system**.

These are the most versatile — always assign them to your highest-priority fellows (120 apt UR carries).
