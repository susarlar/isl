# Advanced Strategies — Level 45+ Guide

## The Single-Carry Meta

The most important strategic concept in Isekai: Slow Life endgame.

### Why One Main Fellow?
Multipliers compound together. Spreading multipliers across 5 fellows gives you 5 moderately strong fellows. Stacking all multipliers on ONE fellow gives you one exponentially powerful fellow + 4 decent ones.

**Math proof:**
- 5 fellows each with 2 multipliers of 1.2x: Each gets 1.2 x 1.2 = 1.44x. Total multiplied power across 5 = 7.2x base
- 1 fellow with 10 multipliers of 1.2x: Gets 1.2^10 = 6.19x. Plus 4 fellows at 1.0x. Total = 10.19x base

**The single carry wins by 41%** even in this simplified example. In practice, the gap is larger because multipliers aren't evenly distributed.

### Choosing Your Carry

| Priority | Fellow | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1st | 120 apt UR (Neptune, Amaterasu, Sunna, Leon, Orivita) | Highest base scaling |
| 2nd | 100+ apt SSR+ (Hermes, Mescal) | Strong if no UR available |
| 3rd | 75 apt SSR with strong skills (Archdemons) | Viable but lower ceiling |

### Carry Checklist
- [ ] Highest level in roster
- [ ] First to limit break
- [ ] All Skill Pearls (Supreme first)
- [ ] Best artifacts (matched type, reforged)
- [ ] Highest awakening stars
- [ ] Best costumes
- [ ] Advanced Blessing focused here
- [ ] Stella category matches carry

## Multiplier Stacking Order

Invest in this order for maximum returns:

### 1. Awakening (Highest Priority)
- Each star = +10% multiplicative
- Compounds with everything
- Gate requirement (4★ needs 15 three-star fellows) means plan early

### 2. Stella
- Category-wide boost amplifies carry AND roster
- Difficult to acquire (character fragments) but very high value
- Focus on the Stella character matching your carry's category

### 3. Artifacts
- Multiplicative bonus
- Must be reforged (common mistake)
- Match type to fellow for full benefit
- Level with Magic Ore — every 10 ore = +1 aptitude

### 4. Advanced Blessing
- Percentage-based, scales with everything
- Prioritize over Fellow Blessing at level 45+
- Level cap 700, requires Blessing Points from dating

### 5. Skill Pearls (Supreme Talent)
- Directly increases Aptitude → feeds into base power
- Supreme Talent gives +3 apt per level, caps at 900
- This is additive to base, but base feeds all multipliers

### 6. Costumes
- Passive aptitude gain, no cost to equip
- Level with duplicates
- Collect all, focus leveling on carry's costumes

### 7. Level + Limit Break
- Additive base increase
- Important but lower priority than multiplier stacking
- Push to next limit break when crystals available

## Resource Allocation Guide

### Skill Pearls
- 100% to main carry until Supreme Talent is maxed
- Then split between building operators

### Acquaint Stones
- First 3: Main carry to 1★
- Next: Push carry to 3★
- Then: Spread across 15+ fellows to 3★ (for 4★ gate)
- Finally: Push carry to 4★+

### Crystal Ores
- Main carry's limit breaks first
- Then spread across roster for leveling

### Magic Ore
- Main carry's artifacts exclusively until maxed

### Blessing Points
- Advanced Blessing on carry's family booster first
- Then spread Fellow Blessing across roster

## Guild Optimization (Level 45+ Guilds)

### Head Office
- Deploy your strongest 30 fellows across 6 types
- Earnings boost applies to ALL guild members
- Coordinate with guild: ensure all 6 types are covered

### Large Commissions
- Higher guild level = better commissions
- Pool strong fellows for commission power thresholds
- Guild Coins from commissions → buy Crystal Ores in exchange shop

### Random Requests
- Complete daily for Guild Coins
- UR rarity requests give best rewards but require strongest fellows

## Common Mistakes at Level 45+

1. **Spreading Acquaint Stones evenly** — Stack on carry first
2. **Ignoring artifact reforging** — No reforge = no bonus
3. **Leveling Fellow Blessing over Advanced Blessing** — % > flat at this stage
4. **Mismatching artifact types** — Brave artifact on Inspiring fellow wastes type bonus
5. **Ignoring Fish Combinations** — Free permanent power many players miss
6. **Not planning for 4★ gate** — 15 three-star fellows takes months to build
7. **Investing in <60 aptitude fellows** — They're permanently behind
8. **Skipping Stella** — Category-wide multiplier is one of the strongest in the game
