# Advanced Strategies — Level 45+ Guide

> See `power-formula.md` for the verified power calculation. This doc applies that formula to strategic decisions.

## The Core Formula (Repeat for Reference)

```
Total Power = (Base × (1 + Σ%) + ΣFlat) × (1 + FinalMultiplier%)
Base = Aptitude × Level Multiplier
```

**All % bonuses are ADDITIVE.** Fish + Family + Artifacts + Stars + Museum + all other % sources literally sum together into one big number, then multiply Base.

## The Single-Carry Meta (Still Valid — Different Reasoning)

### Why It Still Works

Even though % bonuses don't compound, stacking everything on one fellow still wins massively because:

1. **One fellow accumulates ALL the % sources** — your main carry ends up with 8,000%+ while others sit at 500–2,000%
2. **One fellow accumulates ALL the aptitude sources** — Skill Pearls, costumes, artifact materia, family stella aptitude
3. **Both feed the same formula multiplicatively** (aptitude × level × sum%)

### Real Gap Example (Amaterasu vs Woolf from verified data)
- **Amaterasu**: Aptitude 32,823 × Base 508M × (1 + 81) = 48B
- **Woolf**: Aptitude 4,736 × Base 49M × (1 + 47) = 2.5B
- **Gap: ~19x**

Amaterasu has 7x the aptitude AND 1.7x the % sum, and those gaps multiply.

## Fish Investment Is Critical

With +2,624% from Fish on Amaterasu, fish is THE biggest source of % power. If you're not maxing fish, you're leaving massive power on the table.

### Priority Fish to Max
1. **Ocean top 5** (All Fellow %) — Sperm Whale, Giant Squid, Helicoprion, Smooth Hammerhead, Mosasaurus
2. **Northrealm category %** (matching your main type) — Barreleye/Snow Leopard/Narwhal/Fin Whale/Sea Angel
3. **Drakenberg category %** — Pirarucu/Gold Pirarucu/Star Anglerfish/Drakenberg Monster
4. **Village category %** — Axolotl (if Inspiring)
5. **Aptitude + % combo fish** — Both the aptitude normal skill AND the gold crown %

## Family Stella Is #2

With +2,019% from Family on Amaterasu, every family that blesses your main carry should have their Stella pushed. Each maxed UR Family Stella adds +120% to the blessed fellow.

### Family Stella Priority
1. **UR families blessing your main carry** — 120% power + 220 aptitude + 50 cap per max
2. **SSR families blessing your main carry** — 33% power + 56 aptitude per max
3. **Lancelot and Hestia** — family-Stella-based custom slots for any fellow

## Artifacts Are #3

With +788% from the top artifact (Diablo Doll Lv436 materia Lv80), artifacts are a huge endgame investment.

### Artifact Priority
1. **Magic Lamp or Kanna Plush** — 4 materia slots for max stacking
2. **Level artifact to 400+** — Skill count scales with artifact level
3. **Materia Lv 80** — +3,515 aptitude observed
4. **Roll skills with Refine Materia** — Event-exclusive, save for main carry
5. **Breakthrough Materia activates Lv20 materia slots** — use these for random power boosts

## Don't Over-Prioritize Awakening

**Awakening only adds +50% total at endgame** (6★). That's a rounding error compared to Fish's +2,624% or Family's +2,019%.

**But Awakening DOES unlock:**
- Aptitude skills per star
- Access to gates (required for progression)
- Unique fellow-specific bonuses

So push Awakening, but don't prioritize it over Fish/Family/Artifact investment.

## Resource Allocation Rule of Thumb

**For every resource, ask:**
- Does it contribute to aptitude? (Highest priority — multiplies through the huge % sum)
- Does it contribute to a big % source (Fish, Family, Artifacts)? (Second priority)
- Does it contribute to a small % source (Awakening, Expo, Compendium)? (Third)
- Does it contribute to flat power? (Lowest priority at endgame)

## Common Mistakes Corrected

1. ~~"Multipliers compound exponentially"~~ → **Additive, they sum**
2. ~~"Awakening is the biggest multiplier"~~ → **Fish is, by far**
3. ~~"Stella is the biggest multiplier"~~ → **Second to Family**
4. ~~"Spreading multipliers wastes them"~~ → **Spreading or stacking are mathematically equivalent per source, but stacking gives one fellow the combined sum**
5. ~~"Addends become rounding errors"~~ → **Building Service Level (+157M) and Item (+467M) are significant even at endgame**
