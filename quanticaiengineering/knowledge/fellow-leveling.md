| Exp Stone M.  | 10,000      | Mid-priority fellows             |

### Other EXP Sources
- **School Education:** Passive EXP over time, uses Education Points (1 EP per 5 min recovery)
- **Adventure Progression:** EXP from clearing stages

## Village Earning Bonuses from Leveling

Fellows unlock Village Earning Bonuses at specific milestones:
- **Level 50:** First earning bonus unlocked
- **Level 200:** Second earning bonus unlocked

These are passive boosts to building operations — important for Gold income scaling.

## Advanced Leveling Strategy

### For Your Main Carry
1. Push to next limit break ASAP — the multiplier matters more than individual levels
2. Always have enough crystals banked for the next break before spending EXP
3. The power-per-aptitude acceleration means levels 500+ give dramatically more power per level than early levels

### For Your Roster
1. Get all fellows to at least Level 200 for the Village Earning Bonus
2. Spread EXP Stones evenly — the additive nature means you get more total power from leveling 10 fellows from 100→200 than pushing one fellow from 500→600
3. Prioritize fellows that match your building types for economic returns

### Crystal Farming Priority
- **Event shops** — Usually the cheapest source
- **Guild Exchange** — Daily purchases
- **Sea Fishing ranking** — Competitive but rewarding
- **Fountain of Wishes** — Low priority (2.1% Acquaint Fragment rate, not crystals)

## Power Scaling Example

A fellow with 100 Aptitude:
- Level 100: 100 x 925 = **92,500 base power**
- Level 300: 100 x 3,362 = **336,200 base power** (3.6x from 200 levels)
- Level 500: 100 x 7,590 = **759,000 base power** (2.3x from 200 more levels)
- Level 700: 100 x 13,680 = **1,368,000 base power** (1.8x from 200 more levels)

A fellow with 120 Aptitude at the same levels:
- Level 300: 120 x 3,362 = **403,440** (+20% over 100 apt)
- Level 700: 120 x 13,680 = **1,641,600** (+20% over 100 apt)

**Higher base aptitude compounds with everything else.** This is why UR fellows with 120 aptitude (Amaterasu, Sunna, Leon, Neptune, Orivita) are the best long-term investments.
